THE ANCIENT GAME

Use WASD (arrow keys also work) to explore the ancient maze and gather all eight mysterious gem, you can collect all the treasure along the way but be wary because you're not alone, don't try to touch them or you shall perish, be quick or you'll be buried with the precious gems forever!
